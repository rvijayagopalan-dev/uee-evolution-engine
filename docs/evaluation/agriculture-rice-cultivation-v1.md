# What Is Missing?

If we use rice cultivation as a stress test, the main gaps in UEEOS v1.0 are:

1. **Biological Evolution Layer**

   * Genetics
   * Seed breeding
   * Crop evolution

2. **Ecological Layer**

   * Soil microbiome
   * Watershed systems
   * Biodiversity

3. **Physical Systems Layer**

   * Physics of water
   * Climate dynamics
   * Energy flows

4. **Scientific Discovery Engine**

   * Hypothesis generation
   * Experimental design
   * Agricultural research

5. **Regenerative Systems Engine**

   * Carbon sequestration
   * Circular agriculture
   * Ecosystem restoration

These additions would likely form the basis of a future **UEEOS v2.0 Comprehensive Edition**, making it applicable not only to enterprises and civilizations but also to biological and ecological systems.
