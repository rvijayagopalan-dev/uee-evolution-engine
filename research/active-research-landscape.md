## Reading it for this program

- **Most aligned with the falsifiable PhD (scope 1):** IS/design-science maturity
  community (F) + complexity empirics (SFI, CSH).
- **Source of a formal invariant (scope 2):** Friston/active inference (B) is the
  front-runner; assembly theory & computational mechanics are measurable challengers.
- **Where scope 4 is actually attempted:** DeepMind + AGI community (C) — far more
  resourced, so the edge here is **empirical domain-invariance in real socio-technical
  systems**, not out-building them on formalism.
- **Realistic supervisor / collaboration targets:** SFI (complexity), Stockholm
  Resilience Centre (adaptive societal systems), an IS design-science group (maturity
  rigor), or a UCL/active-inference group (if going formal early).
