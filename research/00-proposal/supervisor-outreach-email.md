# Supervisor Outreach Email (Template)

*Short, tailored cold email to a prospective supervisor. Goal: one question answered —
is this a gap worth a PhD, and would you (or someone you know) supervise it?
Keep it under ~150 words; attach the [executive summary](./executive-summary.md).*

> Tailoring rules: replace every [bracket]; the **second paragraph must cite their
> actual work** (generic flattery is obvious). One ask, one attachment. No proposal
> dump — the executive summary is enough.

---

## Subject
`PhD enquiry — a validated, cross-domain maturity model of adaptive capability`

## Body

Dear Professor [Surname],

I'm an enterprise architect and AI strategy leader exploring a PhD, and I'd value
your view on whether the idea is a genuine research gap.

Every industry has "maturity models" (CMMI, data maturity), but none has been shown to
be **domain-invariant, empirically validated, and predictive of adaptation outcomes**.
I propose to test an 11-level maturity model of adaptive capability across three
unrelated domains — designed to be *falsifiable* (a pre-registered null if it fails to
transfer). Your work on [their specific topic — e.g., maturity-model design science /
complex adaptive systems / organizational resilience] is closely related, especially
[one concrete point].

A one-page summary is attached. **Would you consider supervising this — or, if it's not
your area, could you point me to who might?**

Thank you for your time.

[Name]
[role / LinkedIn / email]

---

## Notes
- **Best targets** (see [`../active-research-landscape.md`](../active-research-landscape.md)):
  IS design-science groups (maturity rigor), complexity/resilience centres (SFI,
  Stockholm Resilience Centre), or an active-inference group if going formal.
- Send **2–3 tailored** emails, not a mass mailing.
- If no reply in ~2 weeks, one polite follow-up, then move on.
- A "this overlaps existing work X" reply is **valuable** — it sharpens or redirects
  the gap (the systematic review's job, done for free).
