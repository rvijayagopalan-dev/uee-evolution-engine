## G. Where the gap sits (the intersection)

- **Direct PhD precedents to study closely:** Legg (A), Shalizi (B), Pei Wang (A).
- **The lineage to out-argue:** von Bertalanffy (D) + CMMI (F) — home of the
  "isn't this just GST / CMMI?" rebuttal.
- **The empty cell:** maturity models (F) are *validated but single-domain*;
  CAS / universal-intelligence work (A, C) is *cross-domain but rarely empirically
  validated as a maturity scale*. **This program's contribution sits in their
  intersection** — the bottom-right cell of the review's 2×2 matrix
  (cross-domain + validated + predictive).
