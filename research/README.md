# Research — Domain-Invariant Maturity Engine (PhD Track)

This folder houses the **doctoral research program** built on the grounded core of
the UEEOS Rice Cultivation evaluation. Where [`docs/evaluation/`](../docs/evaluation/)
holds the preliminary work (vision, proposal, instrument, dry run), this folder is
where that becomes a full PhD: literature, theory, methodology, studies, data,
publications, and thesis.

> **Working thesis claim.** *A single, domain-invariant maturity meta-model can
> describe and predict adaptation outcomes across unrelated domains — and can be
> proven wrong.* The contribution is the **domain-invariance + predictive
> validity**, not the ladder itself.

## Scope boundary (carried over, non-negotiable)

- **In scope:** the engineering-grounded ladder (UEEOS v1–v20), made measurable and falsifiable.
- **Out of scope:** the metaphysical tiers (v21+). They stay in `docs/evaluation/` as
  vision and are *not* presented as empirical claims — including them would undermine
  the work in review and in a viva.
